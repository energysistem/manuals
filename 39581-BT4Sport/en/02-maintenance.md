## Care and maintenance

* Do not expose your *Energy Wireless BT Sport* to shock, dust, humidity or high temperatures.
* Do not use abrasive or corrosive cleaning products.
* Do not place your *Energy Wireless BT Sport* close to devices that emit strong magnetic fields.
* Keep your earphones clean and dust them with a soft dry cloth.
* Do not disassemble the device. Any repair must be done exlusively by qualified personnel of Energy Sistem Soyntec S.A.
* Be sure to keep your *Energy Wireless BT Sport* turned off while not in use.
* Charge the lithium battery when ran out to lengthen its life.
